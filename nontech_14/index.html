About the Secretary

Hello! My name is Lipika Bagai, and I am the secretary of the Robotics Club. I am excited to share with you some of the amazing projects we have been working on this summer.

Summer Projects

Rover Arm

This project involves creating a robotic arm capable of picking and placing a randomly placed object autonomously.

Gyrobot: Self-Balancing Robot

A two-wheeled self-balancing robot that has locomotion capabilities while maintaining its balance similar to humans.

Space Station: Controls and Communication

To develop and validate the communication and control system for remotely operating the Rover over extensive distances with precise movement and efficient data transfer.

Autonomous Navigation of Mars Rover

To design and simulate the system of autonomous navigation for the mars rover which mainly covers up obstacle detection, obstacle avoidance and path planning to a specific location.
